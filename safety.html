Safety before all

Scooter touring is as safe as cycling on roads. However, we seek to minimise the risk to life and limb for our customers by providing

Safety

  • Tours in low traffic periods (mainly Sunday morning).
  • The best scooters currently available
  • Safety helmets
  • Low risk routes using the existing cycling infrastructure where possible.
  • Optional fully comprehensive insurance.
  • Safety trained guides
